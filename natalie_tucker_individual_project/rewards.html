Rewards You Gain When Shopping

Accomplishments

There's nothing better than gaining a feeling of accomplishment when something you have been working on getting finally becomes a reality. Having that relief and knowledge that you have finally saved enough to purchase an item you have been fantasizing about adding to your collection is such a rewarding feeling.

Altruism

Not only is there a reward in purchasing items for yourself, but there is also the option of giving gifts to those who hold a significance of importance to you. Showing that you are thinking of someone while shopping is a great way to establish that connection of love and importance.
