Buyers Remorse

The process of getting something and later asking yourself if it was worth it is a common thing that happens all to often. Never truly needing an item really is an inconvenience, because it forces you to spend more unnecessary time returning an item in a store.

What is Buyers Remorse?
The feeling you get when you contemplate on why you purchased an item in the first place.
Common questions?
Constantly asking yourself "Do I really need this?" "Will I actually use this?" and among many other questions regarding the item.
